MadKat is the urban clothing brand created by El Rubius, launched in 2020. It combines streetwear style with elements of gamer culture, offering T-shirts, sweatshirts, jackets, and accessories. The brand has collaborated with franchises like Rick and Morty and other creators like Ari Gameplays, establishing itself as a benchmark in urban gamer fashion.
